# Student Guide

Login at `/login` with a student account (seeded: `student1@tartelah.com` / `Student1234!`).

## Getting Started (No Active Subscription Yet)
Go to **التسجيل في برنامج (Enrollment)**: browse packages, pick one, fill the 3-step form, upload your payment proof. Track your request's status (pending → under review → approved/rejected) on the same page. Once approved, an admin activates your subscription and assigns you a teacher — you'll be notified.

## Once Enrolled
- **الرئيسية (Dashboard)** — your stats, next upcoming session, memorization progress at a glance.
- **جدولي الدراسي (Schedule)** — your recurring lesson pattern, day-grouped, today highlighted.
- **حصصي (Sessions)** — upcoming and past sessions; join your lesson via the meeting link shown when it's about to start.
- **الواجبات (Homework)** — submit assignments before their due date; see your grade + teacher feedback once graded.
- **التقييمات (Evaluations)** — scores and notes your teacher has recorded for you.
- **المستويات (Progress)** — your memorization and revision history by surah.
- **السجل الأكاديمي (Academic Record)** — your course enrollments and progress percentage.
- **اشتراكي (Subscription)** — sessions remaining, days until expiry, package details.

## Notifications
The bell icon and **الاشعارات** page show real-time updates for new homework, evaluations, upcoming sessions, and subscription changes. Unread items are visually highlighted; you can mark individually or mark-all-read.

## Attendance & Absence
Your attendance is recorded per session by your teacher after each lesson. If a session goes untouched for several hours, the system automatically flags it (never immediately, always with a grace period) rather than instantly marking you absent — if you believe an absence was recorded in error, contact your teacher or the academy so an admin can correct it (all corrections are logged, nothing is silently changed).

## Settings
Update your profile and password under **الإعدادات**.
