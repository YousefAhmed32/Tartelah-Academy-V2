# Teacher Guide

Login at `/login` with a teacher account (seeded: `teacher1@tartelah.com` / `Teacher1234!`).

## First Steps
1. **الإعدادات (Settings)** — confirm your gender (`معلم`/`معلمة`) is set correctly; it determines your honorific and avatar everywhere you're shown publicly. Add your Zoom/Meet meeting link(s) under **روابط الحصص**.
2. **الرئيسية (Dashboard)** — check the "needs attention" count; it flags your own missed or completed-but-unfinalized sessions from the last 14 days.

## Scheduling a New Student
Once admin assigns you a student (via enrollment approval), go to **الحصص والجداول** → "إنشاء جدول دوري": pick the student, frequency (weekly/biweekly/monthly/custom), days + time, session count, and meeting link. Confirming the wizard auto-generates all sessions — no need to create them one by one.

## Running a Session
- The session becomes actionable up to 60 minutes before its scheduled time.
- **Check in** through the platform when you start (this is a platform readiness signal, not proof you joined the external call — but it's what determines whether you're marked `on_time` or `late`).
- After the lesson: mark the student's attendance, and either **Complete** the session or report a **Delay** if it ran over/under differently than planned.
- You have a grace period (60 min) plus an extended window (180 min) after the scheduled end to finalize attendance without any penalty — late finalization is simply stamped as such for transparency, not blocked.

## Attendance & Homework
- **الحضور والغياب** — mark present/absent/late/excused/left-early/technical-issue per session; attendance stays a draft until finalized.
- **الواجبات** — create assignments, assign to one or more students, review + grade submissions with feedback.
- **التقييمات** — score a student's tajweed/hifz/nazra/behavior/general performance with strengths/improvements notes; shared with the student by default.
- **التقدم الدراسي** — log memorization and revision entries (surah + ayah range + quality rating).

## Payroll
**الأداء (Performance)** shows your own payroll-readiness breakdown — which sessions are payable, non-payable, or pending review, and why. If a session shows `pending_review`, it means the system doesn't yet have enough confidence to classify it automatically; an admin will resolve it, or you can flag it via the session's delay/note fields.
